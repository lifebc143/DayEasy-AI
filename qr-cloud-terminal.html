Key Advantages

Fast, compact, and ready for cloud-based access projects

This all-in-one terminal combines a dual-core Xtensa processor, embedded FreeRTOS system, QR and card recognition, Bluetooth access, OLED feedback, and dry-contact door integration in a compact 86 x 86 mm form factor.

QR / Bluetooth / IC / CPU / NFC HTTP / MQTT / TCP-IP Fast Scan Under 0.2 Seconds

Multi-Credential Recognition

Supports QR code, Bluetooth, IC card, CPU card, and NFC for flexible access deployment.

Fast Reading Performance

Reads codes within 0.2 seconds at a scan distance of 5 to 15 cm with wide scan angles.

Cloud Protocol Ready

Supports HTTP, MQTT, and TCP-IP for connected access control and platform integration.
